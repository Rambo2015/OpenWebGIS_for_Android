OpenWebGIS motto (slogan) - Open, free of charge online geographical information system for all of us.

The opportunity to use all services/functions at opengis.dlinkddns.com (further OpenWebGIS, the site administration) is given to you by the site administration absolutely free according to the User’s Agreement.

You can download your data on the server opengis.dlinkddns.com, getting access to them from any place where there is the Internet. You can use the vast mathematical functional for your data calculation and analyses. Using OpenWebGIS, you get online geographical information system(GIS), which is functionally close to many desktop GIS, into your disposal.

If OpenWebGIS resources are not enough for you or you suppose that some OpenWebGIS services or functions don’t work correctly, then you are always able to contact OpenWebGIS administration at the address OpenWebGIS@yandex.ru in order to describe the problem. As far as possible the new functions will be added or some of them will be improved or existing ones will be extended.

All existing OpenWebGIS functions /services and OpenWebGIS itself were created on the enthusiasm of the site administration, which is simultaneously direct creator, software engineer and programmer. Besides that all your requests and suggestions will be accomplished exclusively on the site administration enthusiasm.

OpenWebGIS project is created exceptionally on personal savings of the site administration opengis.dlinkddns.com and is supported (24 hours functioning maintenance) also on personal creator’s expenses. But personal budget of OpenWebGIS creators isn’t limitless; by the way this project maintenance takes great labor and time efforts. The project is not commercial. There aren’t any advertisements on the site.
